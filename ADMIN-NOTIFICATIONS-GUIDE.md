
# Admin Guide: Sending Push Notifications

## Quick Start

1. Open the app and log in as admin
2. Tap **Notifiche** from the dashboard
3. Choose your notification type
4. Send to all users

## Notification Types

### 1. Spin the Wheel üé∞

**What it does:**
- Sends a notification to all users inviting them to spin the wheel
- Users tap the notification to open the Fidelity screen
- They can spin to win a random coupon

**How to send:**
1. Tap **Invia Notifica Ruota** button
2. Confirm you want to send
3. Done! All users will receive the notification

**Best for:**
- Special promotions
- Slow business days
- Customer engagement campaigns

---

### 2. Custom Broadcast üì¢

**What it does:**
- Sends a custom message to all users
- You write the title and message
- Users tap to open the app

**How to send:**
1. Enter a **Titolo Notifica** (title)
2. Enter a **Messaggio Notifica** (message)
3. Tap **Invia a Tutti gli Utenti**
4. Done!

**Best for:**
- Special offers (e.g., "15% off today!")
- New product announcements
- Holiday greetings
- Important updates

**Tips:**
- Keep titles short (under 50 characters)
- Make messages clear and actionable
- Include a call-to-action (e.g., "Book now!")
- Use emojis to grab attention üéâ

## Best Practices

### ‚úÖ Do's

- **Be timely**: Send during business hours (9 AM - 8 PM)
- **Be relevant**: Only send important updates
- **Be clear**: Make the message easy to understand
- **Be personal**: Use friendly, conversational tone
- **Be consistent**: Don't send too many (max 2-3 per week)

### ‚ùå Don'ts

- **Don't spam**: Too many notifications = users disable them
- **Don't send late**: Avoid notifications after 9 PM
- **Don't be vague**: Be specific about the offer/update
- **Don't overuse**: Save notifications for important things
- **Don't forget to test**: Always check the message before sending

## Troubleshooting

### "Nessun premio configurato"

**Problem:** Can't send spin the wheel notification

**Solution:** 
1. Go to **Ricompense** (Rewards)
2. Add at least one spin wheel prize
3. Make sure it's marked as active
4. Try again

### "Impossibile inviare la notifica"

**Problem:** Notification failed to send

**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try again in a few minutes
3. If problem persists, contact support

### Users Not Receiving Notifications

**Possible reasons:**
1. User hasn't granted notification permissions
2. User disabled notifications in phone settings
3. User's phone is offline
4. Notification was delayed (can take a few minutes)

**What to do:**
- Remind users to enable notifications
- Check if notification appears in their app
- Wait a few minutes and check again

## Tips for Success

### 1. Plan Your Notifications

Create a notification calendar:
- Monday: Appointment reminder
- Wednesday: Product promotion
- Friday: Weekend special offer

### 2. Test First

Before sending to all users:
1. Send to yourself first (if possible)
2. Check how it looks
3. Make sure links/actions work

### 3. Time It Right

**Best times to send:**
- Morning: 9-11 AM (planning their day)
- Lunch: 12-2 PM (break time)
- Evening: 5-7 PM (after work)

**Avoid:**
- Early morning (before 9 AM)
- Late night (after 9 PM)
- During typical work hours (10 AM - 4 PM)

### 4. Seasonal Campaigns

**Examples:**
- New Year: "New year, new look! 15% off"
- Summer: "Beat the heat with our cooling products"
- Holidays: "Holiday special - Book now!"
- Back to school: "Fresh cut for a fresh start"
